About IJBRSS

IJBRSS (Internantional Journal of Business Research and Social Sciences ) is a prestigious scholarly publication at the forefront of business and technology research. With a commitment to rigorous peer review and open access, IJBRSS offers valuable insights, fostering collaboration among researchers, academics, practitioners, and policymakers to shape the future of these dynamic fields.
